Ship's Log

A diary by Cap. Crit 'Red Beard' McGilicutty

Pirate Layout

Captain Crit "Red Beard" McGilicutty, a fearless mariner born on the rugged shores of Dragon's Cove, embarked on a legendary pirate career. With a flaming red beard that matched his fiery spirit, he roamed the treacherous waters of the Roaring Abyss and the tempestuous Serpent's Tail Sea. His crowning achievement was the harrowing battle against the monstrous Kraken of the Ebon Maw, a fierce encounter that left him scarred but triumphant.

McGilicutty met his end at the age of fifty, having weathered countless storms and vanquished mythical sea beasts. His legacy endured as a swashbuckling icon, celebrated by seafarers across the Seven Tides for his indomitable spirit and unyielding pursuit of adventure on the high seas.
